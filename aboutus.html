Our Story

Aberfoyle Inn is a traditional Scottish inn, proudly located in the heart of the stunning Loch Lomond & The Trossachs National Park. A place where comfort, character, and genuine hospitality come together.

We are ideally situated to be your first choice of base for exploring Loch Lomond, The Trossachs, and the wider landscapes of Scotland. Whether you’re walking, hiking, cycling, fishing, or simply exploring and enjoying the local area — you’ll find everything right on our doorstep.

After a day of adventure, you can always expect the warmest of welcomes at Aberfoyle Inn. Friendly faces, relaxed surroundings, and an atmosphere that feels just like home.

Child & Dog Friendly

We proudly welcome families and four-legged friends. Children are welcome until 9pm — and your dog can stay right through to last orders!

Our aim is simple — to make your stay in Scotland as pleasant and memorable as possible. If you have any questions, queries, or special requirements, please don’t hesitate to get in touch.
