Go Ape
Aberfoyle

An unforgettable forest adventure set high among the ancient woodlands of Queen Elizabeth Forest Park.

From soaring zip lines to suspended walkways and breathtaking views across Loch Lomond & The Trossachs, Go Ape Aberfoyle is where adrenaline meets raw Scottish nature.

Discover the Experience →

High Above the Forest Floor

Glide across some of the longest zip lines in the UK, suspended above rivers, waterfalls and towering pine trees. Each course is designed to challenge, excite and inspire.

A Challenge for All

Whether you’re an experienced adventurer or a first-timer, Go Ape offers courses suited to different confidence levels, all fully supervised by trained instructors.

More Than Just Thrills

Not feeling brave? Enjoy peaceful forest trails, wildlife viewpoints and the relaxed Lodge Café — perfect for spectating with a coffee in hand.
